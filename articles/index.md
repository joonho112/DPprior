# Articles

### All vignettes

- [Complete API Reference with
  Examples](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/api-reference.md):

  A comprehensive reference for all DPprior package functions, organized
  by category with detailed signatures, parameters, return values, and
  usage examples.

- [The Applied Researcher's Guide to Cluster-Count
  Elicitation](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/applied-guide.md):

  A comprehensive guide for applied researchers on eliciting Gamma
  hyperpriors for the Dirichlet Process concentration parameter α based
  on prior beliefs about the number of clusters.

- [Case Studies: Multisite Trials and
  Meta-Analysis](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/case-studies.md):

  Real-world applications of DPprior in educational multisite trials,
  medical meta-analysis, and large-scale policy evaluation.

- [Diagnosing Your Prior: Avoiding Unintended
  Consequences](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/diagnostics.md):

  Learn to verify that your elicited Gamma hyperprior behaves as
  intended across all relevant dimensions, not just the number of
  clusters.

- [Beyond Cluster Counts: Dual-Anchor Elicitation for Weight
  Control](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/dual-anchor.md):

  Learn to control both expected cluster count AND weight concentration
  using the dual-anchor framework for Dirichlet Process prior
  elicitation.

- [DPprior: Why Prior Elicitation
  Matters](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/introduction.md):

  An introduction to the DPprior package for principled elicitation of
  Gamma hyperpriors on the Dirichlet Process concentration parameter α,
  with emphasis on its utility in low-information settings such as
  multisite trials and meta-analyses.

- [Quick Start: Your First Prior in 5
  Minutes](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/quick-start.md):

  Learn how to elicit your first Gamma hyperprior for α in a Dirichlet
  Process model in just 5 minutes using the DPprior package.

- [From Poisson Proxy to NegBin: The A1 Approximation
  Theory](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/theory-approximations.md):

  A rigorous treatment of the Poisson approximation for large J, scaling
  constant selection, the Negative Binomial gamma mixture, and the
  closed-form A1 inverse mapping for Gamma hyperprior elicitation.

- [Exact Moment Matching: The A2 Newton
  Algorithm](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/theory-newton.md):

  A rigorous treatment of the Newton-Raphson algorithm for exact moment
  matching, including score-based Jacobian derivation, convergence
  analysis, numerical safeguards, and comparison with the A1 closed-form
  approximation.

- [Mathematical Foundations: The K Distribution and Gamma
  Hyperpriors](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/theory-overview.md):

  A comprehensive treatment of the mathematical foundations underlying
  the DPprior package, including the exact distribution of the number of
  clusters K_J, moment calculations, and the theory behind Gamma
  hyperprior elicitation.

- [Technical Deep Dive: Stirling Numbers and the Antoniak
  Distribution](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/theory-stirling.md):

  A comprehensive treatment of unsigned Stirling numbers of the first
  kind, numerically stable log-space computation, and their application
  to the Antoniak distribution for exact computation of P(K_J = k \| α).

- [Weight Distributions: w₁, ρ, and the Dual-Anchor
  Framework](https://joonho112.github.io/DPprior/articles/theory-weights.md):

  A comprehensive treatment of stick-breaking weight distributions under
  Gamma hyperpriors, the I_c functional for closed-form moments, and the
  mathematical foundations of dual-anchor elicitation.
